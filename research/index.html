Automatic Detection of Speech under Cold

Language: Python
Index Terms: Strength Modeling, Speech under Cold, Computational Paralinguistics

We deal with the Cold sub-challenge proposed in the INTERSPEECH 2017 COMPUTATIONAL PARALINGUISTICS CHALLENGE. Base on the strength modeling, in addition to conventionally extract feature of audio signal by standard OPENSMILE, we do further fusion on not only feature-level fusion and decision-level fusion, but also fusion the result from multiple sub-dictionary whithin the framework of GMM model followed by Fisher Vector encoding. Moreover, we combine the above result of fusion with eGeMAPS functional features, and achieve 70.2% and 65.5% on development and test set provided by 2017 ComPareE, respectively.

For more details in the approach mentioned above, please refer to Interspeech 2017 ComPareE in my blog
